Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Company: Blackthorne Groupe LLC

This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or usage data. All functionality operates entirely on-device.

1. Overview

Blank Network Monitor is designed with privacy as a core principle. All network analysis and functionality are performed locally on your device.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or usage data, including:

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No IP addresses or device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking data
  • No background data collection

3. Local Processing Only

All network analysis is performed locally on your device.

  • Data is processed in real-time
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No remote monitoring or external processing occurs

The app may display domain-level network activity (such as website domains). This information is processed in real-time and is not collected, stored, or transmitted by the Company.

4. Data Storage

The application does not store personal data.

  • Network activity is displayed only during active use
  • Data is processed in real-time and not retained by the application

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

  • No service providers
  • No analytics platforms
  • No advertising networks

6. Exported Data

The app allows users to export domain-level network data.

  • Exporting is strictly user-initiated
  • Exported data remains entirely under the user’s control
  • The application does not transmit exported data

7. Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, exposure risk is minimized. All processing occurs locally on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

The application does not collect any personal data from users, including children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date on this page.
